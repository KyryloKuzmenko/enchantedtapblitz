Game Description

Enter a World Torn Asunder by Darkness.
Rebuild It, One Puzzle at a Time.

The Enchanted World is a tile-sliding puzzle adventure wrapped in an emotional tale of healing, courage, and wonder. Guide a brave little fairy through a devastated world, solving intricate handcrafted puzzles to restore magic to forests, deserts, meadows, and even a surreal futuristic landscape.

Every move brings you closer to uncovering forgotten beauty. Every solved puzzle is a step toward hope. This game was lovingly created by artists with a background in film and animation to deliver a story told entirely through mesmerizing visuals and an evocative soundtrack. Headphones recommended.
