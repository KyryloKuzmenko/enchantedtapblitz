FAQ

  • Is the game really free?

    You can play the beginning for free. Unlocking the full adventure requires a one-time purchase.

  • Are there ads?

    No. The game is fully ad-free for a pure, immersive experience.

  • How many levels are in the game?

    There are over 30 puzzles spread across 9 diverse realms, plus bonus seasonal content.

  • Do I need the internet to play?

    The game is fully offline once installed. Enjoy your journey anytime, anywhere.

  • What makes the game unique?

    No dialogue, no text—just mood, animation, puzzles, and music. It’s an emotional visual experience unlike any other.
